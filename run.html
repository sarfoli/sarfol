RUNNING JOURNEY


So I decided to sign myself up for a half marathon in Big Sur California for April 2023. That's 11 miles, not to be mistaken for 11km. My longest run prior to signing up for this thing was 10km. So I was really being spontaneous lol.... I've been tracking my runs with my Apple watch and on Strava. My longest run to this date is 11.13km, wrecked my leg after this though lol. My favourite run location: harbourfront to humber bay or woodbine beach and my favourite running conditions are sunny && cool (7-14 degrees). Fun fact: my feeling of accomplishment comes from seeing my resting HR lower. My goal resting HR is 45bpm. Before I took a hiatus from running my lowest resting HR was 44. Highest HR when running was over 200bpm!

How I Prep for a Run

The night before I focus on drinking lots of water and trying to get good sleep. I'm not great at either :). The morning of my run, I wake up nice and early (and struggle while trying). I'll drink black coffee and eat some light carbs (usually a rice cake with peanut butter and honey, maybe a fruit), and I'll attempt to chug another glass of water. In the meantime, I try to be productive while waiting for the food to digest. And most importantly, I stretch (before & after)!!!
