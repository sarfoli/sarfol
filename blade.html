B L A D I N G

BLADING

I started rollerblading as a child, but stopped after elementary school. At the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, I picked it back up to stay active and have something to do. My longest blade is 40km along lakeshore from victoria park entrance to humber bay bridge and back (a three hour blade). My least favourite blade is urban and downhill. I see my life flash infront of my eyes everytime my wheels catch tar or cracks. Glad I picked it back up though! Blading through the downtown core during twilight makes the fear of falling or getting hit worth it.
